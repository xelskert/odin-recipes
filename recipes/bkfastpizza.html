Breakfast Pizza

Who says you can't have pizza for breakfast?! This recipe is a favorite in our house. I mean, how can you have a bad day when it starts with pizza?

We will share the basics in this recipe but it is completely customizable! Add peppers, sauteed onions, hot sauce, or anything else you can think of! The choice is yours!

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Eggs
  • Bacon
  • Marinara Sauce

Steps

  1. Roll dough out and spread it across a baking sheet
  2. Bake it about half way and then take it out of the oven
  3. Add marinara sauce, eggs, and bacon crumbles
  4. Finish baking and serve when cooled
