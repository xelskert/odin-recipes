Fantasy Fudge

It's almost time for Christmas which means it's time for some yummy fudge! This simple recipe can be done in less than 20 mins and will make for a great snack for the whole family.

We make this fudge recipe every year which is why it always reminds us of the holidays! We kept the recipe simple and you can add any ingredient you'd like! Some yummy options would be mini marshmallows, caramel, nuts, or cookie crumbles!

Ingrediets

  • 1 bag of chocolate chips
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 jar of jet puffed marshmallows
    •

    Steps

    1. In a large saucepan heat chocolate chips and condensed milk until fully melted
    2. Add jet puff in, making sure to stir constantly
    3. Once everything is mixed, carefully pour it until a 9" x 13" pan lined with wax paper
